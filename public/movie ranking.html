Top 3 favourite movies

Moon

Director: Duncan Jones

This psychological sci-fi film tells the story of a man nearing the end of his three-year shift working on a lunar base, only to discover shocking truths about his mission.

Arrival

Denis Villeneuve,

Arrival (2016) is a science fiction film directed by Denis Villeneuve, based on the short story Story of Your Life by Ted Chiang. It stands out for its contemplative approach to first contact with extraterrestrial life, focusing on language, communication, and time perception, rather than action or conflict.

District 9

Neill Blomkamp

District 9 (2009) is a sci-fi action film directed by Neill Blomkamp, produced by Peter Jackson, and is set in an alternate-reality Johannesburg, South Africa. The movie blends science fiction with social commentary, tackling themes of xenophobia, apartheid, and discrimination through the lens of an alien refugee crisis.
